Here to help with any questions about the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a new placement?

Simply tap on a body zone on the home screen diagram. A confirmation sheet will appear where you can confirm the placement or add a note.

How are recommendations calculated?

Our algorithm looks at the time elapsed since each site was last used and your overall usage balance to suggest the most "rested" area.

Can I add my own body sites?

Yes! Go to the Settings tab, where you can add unlimited custom sites with personalized icons.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored 100% locally on your iPhone. We never see or collect your health information.
