Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

OmniSite Tracker ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a priority.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter into the App, including placement logs and notes, is stored locally on your device only.

No Accounts

The App does not require user registration or accounts. There are no servers storing your information.

No Tracking

The App does not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or any form of user tracking.

No Third Parties

The App does not share data with any third parties.

Data Storage

Your placement data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework on your device. This data is included in your device backups (iCloud or local) if you have backups enabled.

Data Deletion

You can delete all App data by deleting the App from your device.

Contact
